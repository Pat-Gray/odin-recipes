Panang Curry

Description

Tofu Panang Curry is a rich and creamy Thai dish featuring tofu simmered in a velvety Panang curry sauce. This sauce, made from a blend of aromatic spices, coconut milk, and Panang curry paste, imparts a mild yet flavorful heat with notes of lemongrass, kaffir lime, and galangal. The tofu absorbs the fragrant curry sauce, while vegetables like bell peppers and bamboo shoots add texture and freshness. Garnished with fresh basil and served with jasmine rice, this dish offers a delightful balance of savory, spicy, and slightly sweet flavors.

Ingredients

  • Firm Tofu
  • Panang Curry Paste
  • Coconut Cream
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Bell Pepper
  • Carrot
  • Bamboo Shoots
  • Brown Sugar
  • Fish Sauce
  • Fresh Basil

Steps

  1. Prepare Tofu
  2. Cook Curry Paste
  3. Add coconut cream
  4. Add Vegetables
  5. Combine Tofu and Flavourings
  6. Garnish and Serve
