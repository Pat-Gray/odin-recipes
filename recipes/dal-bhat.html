Dah Bhat

Description

Dal Bhat is a traditional South Asian dish consisting of lentil soup (dal) served with steamed rice (bhat). Popular in countries like India, Nepal, and Bangladesh, it is a staple meal known for its nourishing and comforting qualities. The dal is typically made with various lentils cooked with spices like turmeric, cumin, and coriander, offering a rich, hearty flavor. Paired with rice, it forms a balanced, protein-rich meal. Dal Bhat is often accompanied by vegetables, pickles, and yogurt, making it a versatile and beloved comfort food.

Ingredients

  • Lentils
  • Rice
  • Water
  • Tumeric Powder
  • Cumin Seeds
  • Salt
  • Ghee
  • Onion
  • Garlic

Steps

    Cook the Lentils
    Prepare the rice
    Season the rice
    Serve
