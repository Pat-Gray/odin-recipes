Soba Noodles

Description

This soba noodle dish features a delightful blend of nutty, buckwheat noodles stir-fried with crisp, fresh vegetables and tender strips of chicken. Tossed in a savory sauce made from soy, garlic, and a hint of ginger, each bite offers a satisfying balance of rich umami and subtle sweetness. Garnished with sesame seeds and scallions, this dish is both comforting and light, showcasing the versatility and delicate flavor of traditional Japanese soba noodles.

Ingredients

  • Soba Noodles
  • Tofu
  • Sesame oil
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Capsicum
  • Brocolli
  • Carrot
  • Green Onions
  • Soy Sauce

Steps

  1. Cook the Soba Noodles
  2. Prepare vegetables and tofu
  3. Stir-fried vegetables
  4. Combine other ingredients
  5. Garnish and Serve
